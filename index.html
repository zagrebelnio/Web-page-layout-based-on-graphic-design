Ferrari

Ferrari is one of the most popular and iconic Formula One teams, and its fans are known as the "tifosi". The team's red cars and prancing horse logo are instantly recognizable around the world.

Red Bull

Red Bull Racing is currently one of the most successful teams in Formula One, and is the reigning World Champions in both the Drivers' and Constructors' Championships.

Mercedes

Mercedes has been one of the most successful in Formula One history, winning seven consecutive double Drivers' and Constructors' World Championships from 2014 to 2020.

McLaren

The McLaren F1 Team is the second-oldest team on the grid, behind only Ferrari, and has won 12 Drivers' Championships and 8 Constructors' Championships.
